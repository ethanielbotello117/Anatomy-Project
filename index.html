Skeletal Game

Game Rules

  • - You will be given a hint and both players must guess the correct word as fast as possible
  • - The first person who guessed the correct word earns one point
  • - if neither of the players guesses correctly none of them earn a point and they press "NEXT QUESTION"
  • - When the game ends the person with the highest score wins
